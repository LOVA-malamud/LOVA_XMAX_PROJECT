# 2020 Yamaha XMAX 400 Technical Summary

## Engine & Performance
- **Displacement:** 395cc
- **Type:** Single-cylinder, 4-stroke, Liquid-cooled, SOHC, 4-valve
- **Bore x Stroke:** 83.0 mm x 73.0 mm
- **Compression Ratio:** 10.6 : 1
- **Max Power:** 24.5 kW (32.9 HP) @ 7,000 RPM
- **Max Torque:** 36.0 Nm (3.7 kg-m) @ 6,000 RPM
- **Fuel System:** Fuel Injection
- **Ignition:** TCI

## Chassis & Dimensions
- **Frame:** Steel underbone
- **Front Suspension:** Telescopic fork (110mm travel)
- **Rear Suspension:** Unit Swing (92mm travel)
- **Front Brake:** Hydraulic dual disc, Ø 267 mm
- **Rear Brake:** Hydraulic single disc, Ø 267 mm
- **Front Tire:** 120/70-15
- **Rear Tire:** 150/70-13
- **Total Length:** 2,185 mm
- **Total Width:** 766 mm
- **Total Height:** 1,415 mm
- **Seat Height:** 800 mm
- **Wet Weight:** 210 kg
- **Fuel Tank:** 13 Liters

## Fluids & Maintenance
- **Engine Oil (Periodic):** 1.5 L
- **Engine Oil (With Filter):** 1.6 L
- **Transmission Oil:** 0.25 L
- **Coolant:** 1.1 L
- **Spark Plug:** NGK LMAR8A-9
- **Spark Plug Gap:** 0.8–0.9 mm
- **Idle Speed:** 1,400–1,600 RPM

## Periodic Maintenance Schedule
- **Engine Oil:** Change every 5,000 km
- **Oil Filter:** Replace every 10,000 km
- **V-Belt:** Replace every 20,000 km
- **Air Filter:** Replace every 10,000 km
- **Valve Clearance:** Check every 20,000 km
- **Brake Fluid:** Change every 2 years
